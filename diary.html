About this view

The diary is one LLM-generated render of the last 24 hours of git activity across every public 273v/kaos-* repository the collector tracks. The narrative is produced by a frontier model with an auditor-register system prompt; the lists are the model's surfaced items, bounded at 12 (notable, end-user) or 6 (risk).

The counts above (repos observed, commits in window) come from git log directly and are authoritative regardless of the model's narrative. If the narrative disagrees with the counts, trust the counts. The diary is a navigation aid; the per-package detail pages and the raw snapshot.json remain the evidence path.
